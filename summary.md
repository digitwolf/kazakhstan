# Trip Goal and Parameters

## Trip Goal

Design a scenic, safety-first **family motorcycle "first tour"** in the Pacific Northwest whose purpose is to **build the family's touring skills and a love of touring**, anchored on a brand-new rider. Seven days, round trip from home, leaving July 1, 2026.

The trip should combine an easy, confidence-building start with steadily richer scenery — Puget Sound and a ferry, the whole Oregon Coast, and a finale over two Cascade volcanoes — while keeping every day gentle, breakable, and free of high-speed roads.

## The family

* **Galiya — Rider 2 (sets the pace).** Just earned her motorcycle endorsement; rides her own **Kawasaki W230** (233 cc, happiest 55–65 mph, ~3.4-gal tank). A newer solo rider — her confidence governs the route and daily length; she rides up front. A big foodie; loves the coast, scenery and lighthouses.
* **Ruslan — Rider 1.** Seasoned (BMW R1300GS; a Morocco ADV trip). Rides the GS carrying their son. Not the limiting rider — serve him with riding quality.
* **Aslan — 6, passenger.** Rides pillion with dad. Loves toys/souvenirs, animals, hands-on museums, the volcano and the cheese factory. Can do up to ~6h on a good day but not long days back-to-back.

## Rider & safety priorities

Built around Galiya's new-rider confidence and Aslan's comfort:

* No freeways; quiet backroads, coastal highway and gentle, low-speed mountain passes (Chinook Pass is the "graduation" capstone).
* Short, confidence-building days; the ferry and frequent stops break up Day 1; never two long days in a row.
* Stops every 60–90 minutes; nothing technical, wet-aggressive, or after dark.
* Full child-pillion setup on the GS (fitted helmet/armor, peg reach, top-box backrest, grab strap, intercom).
* Plan fuel around the W230's small tank; pack for coast fog/wind, rain, and alpine chill at Chinook Pass.

## Route

Woodinville → **Edmonds–Kingston ferry** → Hood Canal → **Westport** (overnight) → **Astoria** (overnight) → Cannon Beach → **Tillamook** (cheese, capes) → **Yachats** (★ 2-night base, the Fourth of July) → inland → **Mount St. Helens** (overnight) → **Packwood / Mount Rainier** (overnight) → Paradise → **Chinook Pass** → home. ≈ 970 mi over 7 days.

## Lodging & food

* Family-friendly lodging with secure/easy motorcycle parking; the Yachats oceanfront stay is the splurge. Always confirm parking + passenger rules before booking; book the Fourth-of-July coast nights far ahead.
* Food is part of the fun — the **Coast Food Trail**: Dungeness crab, clam chowder, fresh fish-and-chips, Tillamook cheese & ice cream, coast-town breweries.

## Trip style summary

A gentle, premium family first tour — beautiful coast and mountains, short days, one big rest day, and a brand-new rider who comes home a tourer. Not a mileage challenge or an endurance ride.
