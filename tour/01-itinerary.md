## Day notes

### Day 1 — Arrival & Bike Pickup
*Tokyo / Yokohama · 0 mi · Kanto*

Collect the Africa Twin and CB400X, fit the child's gear, check intercoms and do a gentle shakedown ride. Early night.

[🗺️ Route: Haneda Airport → Yokohama](https://www.google.com/maps/dir/?api=1&origin=Haneda%20Airport,%20Tokyo&destination=Yokohama,%20Japan&travelmode=driving) · see [`destinations/`](destinations/) for yokohama

### Day 2 — Into the Mountains
*Yokohama → Hakone · 60 mi · Hakone*

Easy first leg up to Hakone. Lake Ashi pirate boat and the Hakone Ropeway over the volcanic valley.

[🗺️ Route: Yokohama → Hakone](https://www.google.com/maps/dir/?api=1&origin=Yokohama,%20Japan&destination=Hakone,%20Japan&travelmode=driving) · see [`destinations/`](destinations/) for hakone

### Day 3 — Hakone Rest Day
*Hakone (no transfer) · 0 mi · Hakone*

Open-Air Museum, Owakudani black eggs, and a long onsen soak. No packing, no riding pressure.

[🗺️ Route: Hakone → Owakudani](https://www.google.com/maps/dir/?api=1&origin=Hakone,%20Japan&destination=Owakudani,%20Hakone,%20Japan&travelmode=driving) · see [`destinations/`](destinations/) for hakone

### Day 4 — Izu Coastal Run
*Hakone → Izu (Shimoda) · 55 mi · Izu*

Scenic coastal roads down the Izu Peninsula. Beaches, capes and fresh seafood lunches.

[🗺️ Route: Hakone → Shimoda](https://www.google.com/maps/dir/?api=1&origin=Hakone,%20Japan&destination=Shimoda,%20Shizuoka,%20Japan&travelmode=driving) · see [`destinations/`](destinations/) for izu

### Day 5 — To the Five Lakes
*Izu → Fuji Five Lakes · 80 mi · Fuji*

North toward Kawaguchiko with Mt. Fuji filling the windscreen. Lakeside hotel for the night.

[🗺️ Route: Shimoda → Lake Kawaguchiko](https://www.google.com/maps/dir/?api=1&origin=Shimoda,%20Shizuoka,%20Japan&destination=Lake%20Kawaguchiko,%20Japan&travelmode=driving) · see [`destinations/`](destinations/) for fuji

### Day 6 — Fuji Exploration
*Fuji Five Lakes (light riding) · 25 mi · Fuji*

Lake loop, Oishi Park, cable car viewpoint, and a relaxed afternoon. Optional gentle ride only.

[🗺️ Route: Lake Kawaguchiko → Oishi Park](https://www.google.com/maps/dir/?api=1&origin=Lake%20Kawaguchiko,%20Japan&destination=Oishi%20Park,%20Yamanashi,%20Japan&travelmode=driving) · see [`destinations/`](destinations/) for fuji

### Day 7 — Westward Transfer
*Fuji → Nagoya / Gifu · 165 mi · Chubu*

The longest practical transfer of the trip, kept on wider national roads. Comfortable city hotel.

[🗺️ Route: Lake Kawaguchiko → Nagoya](https://www.google.com/maps/dir/?api=1&origin=Lake%20Kawaguchiko,%20Japan&destination=Nagoya,%20Japan&travelmode=driving) · see [`destinations/`](destinations/) for nagoya

### Day 8 — Bridge to the Sea
*Nagoya → Awaji Island · 145 mi · Setouchi*

Cross toward the Seto Inland Sea via Awaji. Coastal scenery and onion-famous local food.

[🗺️ Route: Nagoya → Awaji Island](https://www.google.com/maps/dir/?api=1&origin=Nagoya,%20Japan&destination=Awaji%20Island,%20Japan&travelmode=driving) · see [`destinations/`](destinations/) for awaji

### Day 9 — Into Iya Valley
*Awaji → Iya / Oboke · 90 mi · Shikoku*

Enter Shikoku's dramatic gorge country. Gentle paved valley roads to a remote onsen ryokan.

[🗺️ Route: Awaji Island → Oboke](https://www.google.com/maps/dir/?api=1&origin=Awaji%20Island,%20Japan&destination=Oboke,%20Tokushima,%20Japan&travelmode=driving) · see [`destinations/`](destinations/) for iya

### Day 10 — Iya Rest Day
*Iya Valley (no transfer) · 25 mi · Shikoku*

Kazurabashi vine bridge, the peeing-boy statue viewpoint, and an Oboke Gorge sightseeing boat.

[🗺️ Route: Oboke → Iya Kazurabashi](https://www.google.com/maps/dir/?api=1&origin=Oboke,%20Tokushima,%20Japan&destination=Iya%20Kazurabashi,%20Japan&travelmode=driving) · see [`destinations/`](destinations/) for iya

### Day 11 — Down to the Coast
*Iya → Kochi · 70 mi · Shikoku*

River valleys open out to the Pacific. Kochi Castle and Katsurahama beach in the evening.

[🗺️ Route: Oboke → Kochi](https://www.google.com/maps/dir/?api=1&origin=Oboke,%20Tokushima,%20Japan&destination=Kochi,%20Japan&travelmode=driving) · see [`destinations/`](destinations/) for kochi

### Day 12 — The Clear River
*Kochi → Shimanto River · 85 mi · Shikoku*

Follow the Shimanto, Japan's last free-flowing clear river, past its low submersible bridges.

[🗺️ Route: Kochi → Shimanto](https://www.google.com/maps/dir/?api=1&origin=Kochi,%20Japan&destination=Shimanto,%20Kochi,%20Japan&travelmode=driving) · see [`destinations/`](destinations/) for shimanto

### Day 13 — Shimanto Rest Day
*Shimanto River (no transfer) · 20 mi · Shikoku*

River canoeing or a sightseeing boat, riverside cycling, and slow rural time by the water.

[🗺️ Route: Shimanto → Shimanto River](https://www.google.com/maps/dir/?api=1&origin=Shimanto,%20Kochi,%20Japan&destination=Shimanto%20River,%20Japan&travelmode=driving) · see [`destinations/`](destinations/) for shimanto

### Day 14 — Castles & Old Towns
*Shimanto → Uwajima / Uchiko → Dogo Onsen · 110 mi · Shikoku*

Uwajima Castle and Uchiko's preserved merchant streets en route to Matsuyama.

[🗺️ Route: Shimanto → Matsuyama](https://www.google.com/maps/dir/?api=1&origin=Shimanto,%20Kochi,%20Japan&destination=Matsuyama,%20Ehime,%20Japan&waypoints=Uwajima,%20Ehime,%20Japan&travelmode=driving) · see [`destinations/`](destinations/) for dogo

### Day 15 — Dogo Onsen Rest Day
*Matsuyama (no transfer) · 10 mi · Shikoku*

Matsuyama Castle by ropeway, then the historic Dogo Onsen bathhouse. Classic onsen evening.

[🗺️ Route: Matsuyama → Dogo Onsen](https://www.google.com/maps/dir/?api=1&origin=Matsuyama,%20Ehime,%20Japan&destination=Dogo%20Onsen,%20Matsuyama,%20Japan&travelmode=driving) · see [`destinations/`](destinations/) for dogo

### Day 16 — Shimanami Kaido
*Matsuyama → Shimanami → Onomichi / Setoda · 95 mi · Setouchi*

The famous island-hopping bridge route across the Seto Inland Sea — gentle, scenic, unforgettable.

[🗺️ Route: Matsuyama → Onomichi](https://www.google.com/maps/dir/?api=1&origin=Matsuyama,%20Ehime,%20Japan&destination=Onomichi,%20Hiroshima,%20Japan&waypoints=Imabari,%20Ehime,%20Japan&travelmode=driving) · see [`destinations/`](destinations/) for shimanami

### Day 17 — Island Rest Day
*Setoda / Onomichi (light riding) · 25 mi · Setouchi*

Setoda temple, lemon groves and gelato, Onomichi's hillside lanes and cat alley. Easy pace.

[🗺️ Route: Onomichi → Setoda](https://www.google.com/maps/dir/?api=1&origin=Onomichi,%20Hiroshima,%20Japan&destination=Setoda,%20Ikuchijima,%20Japan&travelmode=driving) · see [`destinations/`](destinations/) for onomichi

### Day 18 — Canal Town or Art Island
*Onomichi → Kurashiki / Takamatsu · 90 mi · Setouchi*

Kurashiki's willow-lined canal quarter, or a ferry to Naoshima's art islands.

[🗺️ Route: Onomichi → Kurashiki](https://www.google.com/maps/dir/?api=1&origin=Onomichi,%20Hiroshima,%20Japan&destination=Kurashiki,%20Okayama,%20Japan&travelmode=driving) · see [`destinations/`](destinations/) for kurashiki

### Day 19 — The Great Castle
*Kurashiki → Himeji / Kobe · 70 mi · Kansai*

Himeji Castle, Japan's most magnificent, then into Kobe for the night.

[🗺️ Route: Kurashiki → Himeji](https://www.google.com/maps/dir/?api=1&origin=Kurashiki,%20Okayama,%20Japan&destination=Himeji,%20Hyogo,%20Japan&travelmode=driving) · see [`destinations/`](destinations/) for himeji

### Day 20 — Finish in Kansai
*Kobe → Osaka / Kansai · 60 mi · Kansai*

Short final leg into the city. Return the motorcycles and celebrate the journey.

[🗺️ Route: Himeji → Osaka](https://www.google.com/maps/dir/?api=1&origin=Himeji,%20Hyogo,%20Japan&destination=Osaka,%20Japan&travelmode=driving) · see [`destinations/`](destinations/) for osaka

### Day 21 — Buffer & Departure
*Osaka / Kyoto · 0 mi · Kansai*

Flexible buffer day — Kyoto temples or Osaka food and aquarium before flying home.

[🗺️ Route: Osaka → Kyoto](https://www.google.com/maps/dir/?api=1&origin=Osaka,%20Japan&destination=Kyoto,%20Japan&travelmode=driving) · see [`destinations/`](destinations/) for osaka
