# 21-Day Itinerary & Daily Schedule

**Total riding ≈ 1,460 miles (2,349 km) over 21 days.** Every transfer day is now at least ~3 hours in the saddle (≈90+ mi at a relaxed 30 mph pace), broken up by sightseeing, coffee and lunch stops. Rest days stay light.

## The daily routine (riding days)

| Time | | |
|------|---|---|
| 07:00 | ⏰ | Wake up |
| 07:30 | ☕ | Coffee |
| 08:00 | 🍳 | Breakfast |
| 09:00 | 🏍️ | Depart hotel |
| en route | 📸☕🍜📷 | Sightseeing, coffee & lunch stops (placed by where they fall along the route) |
| late afternoon | 🏁 | Arrive, check in |
| evening | ♨️🍽️ | Onsen, then dinner |

Stop times on each day's live page (`day.html?d=N`) are computed from cumulative driving distance + a realistic dwell at each stop, so the schedule tracks the actual route.

## Day notes

### Day 1 — Arrival & Bike Pickup
*Tokyo / Yokohama · 0 mi · Kanto · rest day*

Collect the Africa Twin and CB400X, fit the child's gear, check intercoms and do a gentle shakedown ride. Early night.

**Stops along the way:**
- 🎯 **Minato Mirai 21** — Harbour skyline & the Cosmo Clock wheel
- 🎯 **Cup Noodles Museum** — Make-your-own-noodles fun for kids
- 🍜 **Yokohama Chinatown** — Welcome dinner — buns & dim sum

[🗺️ Day route](https://www.google.com/maps/dir/?api=1&origin=Haneda%20Airport,%20Tokyo&destination=Yokohama,%20Japan&waypoints=Minato%20Mirai%2021%20Yokohama|Cup%20Noodles%20Museum%20Yokohama|Yokohama%20Chinatown&travelmode=driving)

### Day 2 — Into the Mountains
*Yokohama → Hakone · 90 mi · Hakone*

Easy first leg up to Hakone. Lake Ashi pirate boat and the Hakone Ropeway over the volcanic valley.

**Stops along the way:**
- 📸 **Odawara Castle** — Hilltop castle en route
- ☕ **Hakone-Yumoto** — Onsen-town coffee break
- 🍜 **Lake Ashi & Hakone Shrine** — Lakeside torii & pirate boat, soba lunch
- 📷 **Owakudani** — Volcanic valley & black eggs

[🗺️ Day route](https://www.google.com/maps/dir/?api=1&origin=Yokohama,%20Japan&destination=Hakone,%20Japan&waypoints=Odawara%20Castle|Hakone-Yumoto%20Station|Hakone%20Shrine%20Lake%20Ashi|Owakudani&travelmode=driving)

### Day 3 — Hakone Rest Day
*Hakone (no transfer) · 0 mi · Hakone · rest day*

Open-Air Museum, Owakudani black eggs, and a long onsen soak. No packing, no riding pressure.

**Stops along the way:**
- 🎯 **Hakone Open-Air Museum** — Sculpture park with kids' play zones
- 🎯 **Owakudani Ropeway** — Steam vents & black eggs
- 📷 **Hakone Shrine** — Lakeside red torii photo

[🗺️ Day route](https://www.google.com/maps/dir/?api=1&origin=Hakone,%20Japan&destination=Owakudani,%20Hakone,%20Japan&waypoints=Hakone%20Open-Air%20Museum|Owakudani|Hakone%20Shrine&travelmode=driving)

### Day 4 — Izu Coastal Run
*Hakone → Izu (Shimoda) · 90 mi · Izu*

Scenic coastal roads down the Izu Peninsula. Beaches, capes and fresh seafood lunches.

**Stops along the way:**
- 📸 **Manazuru Cape** — Coastal viewpoint
- ☕ **Jogasaki Coast** — Lava cliffs & suspension bridge
- 🍜 **Kawazu** — Kinmedai seafood lunch
- 📷 **Shirahama Beach** — Beach & old port town

[🗺️ Day route](https://www.google.com/maps/dir/?api=1&origin=Hakone,%20Japan&destination=Shimoda,%20Shizuoka,%20Japan&waypoints=Manazuru%20Cape|Jogasaki%20Coast|Kawazu%20Shizuoka|Shirahama%20Beach%20Shimoda&travelmode=driving)

### Day 5 — To the Five Lakes
*Izu → Fuji Five Lakes · 95 mi · Fuji*

North toward Kawaguchiko with Mt. Fuji filling the windscreen. Lakeside hotel for the night.

**Stops along the way:**
- 📸 **Mishima Skywalk** — Japan's longest suspension footbridge, Fuji view
- ☕ **Mishima** — Coffee break
- 🍜 **Oshino Hakkai** — Spring-water village, soba lunch
- 📷 **Lake Kawaguchiko** — Lakeside arrival & Fuji views

[🗺️ Day route](https://www.google.com/maps/dir/?api=1&origin=Shimoda,%20Shizuoka,%20Japan&destination=Lake%20Kawaguchiko,%20Japan&waypoints=Mishima%20Skywalk|Mishima%20Station|Oshino%20Hakkai|Lake%20Kawaguchiko&travelmode=driving)
