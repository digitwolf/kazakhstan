## Destinations

An **Osaka-anchored loop** — start and finish in Osaka. **≈ 1,171 mi (1,884 km)**; 12 transfer days averaging 2h56m.

**Signature foodie thread:** the route threads four of Japan's famous regional ramen cities — Wakayama → Tokushima → Susaki (Kōchi) → Onomichi — celebrated as the **[Ramen Trail](00-overview.md#the-ramen-trail-a-foodie-thread-for-galiya)** (Days 8, 12, 17).

**Getting there/back:** fly **nonstop SEA ⇄ Tokyo**, then ride the **Tōkaidō Shinkansen (~2h30m) Tokyo ⇄ Shin-Osaka** to/from the loop (see [02 — Flights](02-flights.md)). The riding loop is bracketed by city days: a **Tokyo arrival night (Day 0)** + a **full Tokyo museum day (Day 1)** before the **Day-2** pickup, and at the end a **bike return (Day 21)**, a **Super Nintendo World / USJ day (Day 22)** and a **reposition-to-Tokyo day (Day 23)** before flying home **Day 24** — door-to-door **≈ 25 days (Day 0–24)**.
