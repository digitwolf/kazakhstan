## Destinations

A **16-day loop — Day 1 arrival + a 14-day tour body (1 training day + 12 riding days + 1 rest day) + Day 16 departure — guided by [Silk Off Road Tours](https://www.silkoffroadtours.com/motorcycle-tours-kazakhstan-kyrgyzstan)** as a **custom extended edition** of their published 10-day tour: **Almaty → Almaty, ≈ 2,800 km** (planned legs sum 2,809 km), **≈ 60% asphalt / 40% gravel-dirt**, on a rental **Suzuki DR650SE** with ride leader and support vehicle. Planned dates **Sep 5–20, 2026**; indicative price **≈ USD 4,500 (confirm with the operator; the published 10-day tour is USD 3,000 + 500 deposit)**. A training day out of Almaty, the Singing Dune ridden en route to Chundzha, two Kegen/Karkara border crossings, Tian Shan passes to **4,021 m (Suek)**, three yurt-camp nights, a rest day (Day 8) on Issyk-Kul's south shore, then home via the Boom gorge, the Cholpon-Ata petroglyphs, Lake Kaindy and the Kolsai Lakes.

**Signature foodie thread:** lunches and dinners are excluded — by design a **[Silk Road Food Trail](00-overview.md#the-silk-road-food-trail)**: beshbarmak, plov, laghman, manty, shashlik, ashlan-fuu in Karakol, kumis & boorsok in the Song-Kol yurts, smoked lake fish at Cholpon-Ata.

The plan is **anchor-first** (see [03-anchors-and-options.md](03-anchors-and-options.md)): the **★ five anchors** below are 100% must-see with pre-booked nights; the other stops are waypoints used by the chosen route options.
