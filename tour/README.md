## Destinations

A **Woodinville (Seattle) loop** — out down the Washington and Oregon coast, home over the Cascades. **≈ 970 riding miles** over **7 days** (depart **Wed 1 July 2026**, home **Tue 7 July 2026**); average riding day ≈ 3h40m, longest day ≈ 4.1h (the coast→Cascades transfer). Built around **Galiya, a brand-new solo rider** — so it stays **off all freeways and Interstates**, on coastal and mountain two-lanes, with breaks every 60–90 minutes for **Aslan (6)**.

**Signature foodie thread:** the route strings together the coast's best bites — **Westport Dungeness crab → Astoria fish & chips → Tillamook cheese & ice cream → Yachats chowder** — celebrated as the **[Coast Food Trail](00-overview.md#the-coast-food-trail)** (Days 1–4).

**No flights, no rentals:** the family rides their **own** bikes round-trip from home (see [02 — Getting started](02-getting-started.md)). The only crossing is the short **Edmonds–Kingston ferry** on Day 1; the rest day falls on **Sat 4 July** in Yachats, built around the town's **La De Da Parade and bay fireworks**.
