## Day 1 — Ferry, Fjord & First Miles (overnight: Westport, WA)

**Schedule:** Wake ~08:00 · depart Woodinville ~09:25 · waterfront coffee in Edmonds · **10:20 AM Kingston ferry** (summer weekday sailings 9:35 / 10:20 / 11:05 / 11:55 AM, ~30-min crossing) · cruise Hood Canal on US-101 · **oyster lunch ~12:15 at Hama Hama (Lilliwaup)** · arrive Westport ~16:00 · celebratory marina seafood dinner.

### Where to eat

**Coffee — Edmonds waterfront (before the ferry)**
- [Walnut Street Coffee](https://maps.google.com/?cid=9866092700006776997) — 4.7 — top-rated local espresso and pastries; the trip's first stop (grab a pastry for Aslan).
- [Waterfront Coffee Company](https://maps.google.com/?cid=16325238489058569722) — 4.6 — right by the dock for an easy grab-and-go before boarding.

**Lunch — Hama Hama Oyster Saloon, Lilliwaup (the family favourite)**
- [Hama Hama Oyster Saloon](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Hama+Hama+Oyster+Saloon+Lilliwaup+WA) — 4.6 — your favourite Hood Canal stop: just-shucked oysters (raw, grilled, fried) and chowder, right on the water at the oyster farm. **Check the seasonal days/hours before counting on it.**
- **Kid note:** the saloon has fries, chowder and a few non-oyster bites for Aslan, but the menu is oyster-focused — pack a backup snack. (Fallback if it's closed midweek: Billy's Bar & Grill in Aberdeen for burgers.)

**Dinner — Westport marina (the celebratory one)**
- [Blue Buoy](https://maps.google.com/?cid=3139538248414449400) — 4.5 — fresh-off-the-boat Westport seafood right at the harbor.
- [Bennett's Fish Shack](https://maps.google.com/?cid=688763201149346829) — 4.3 — **kid pick:** classic fish & chips, burgers and fries; casual and easy for Aslan.
- [Merino's Seafood Market](https://maps.google.com/?cid=17492398225141631717) — 4.7 — buy fresh Dungeness crab and shrimp cocktails dockside; the most local choice for Ruslan & Galiya.

### Things to do
- **~10:00 — [Edmonds–Kingston Ferry](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Edmonds+Ferry+Terminal+WA):** roll the bikes aboard for the first big moment of the trip.
- **~11:30 — [Hood Canal / US-101](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Hood+Canal+US-101+Washington):** flowing fjord-side sweepers — ideal easy first-day riding, with the Hama Hama oyster lunch right on the route.
- **~16:15 — [Westport Marina](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Westport+Marina+WA):** wander the working harbor; souvenir shops for Aslan.
- **~16:45 — [Westport Light State Park](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Westport+Light+State+Park+WA):** short paved walk to the lighthouse and beach to stretch out after the ride.
