## Day 1 — Tokyo Museum Day (no riding)

A full, kid-focused Tokyo day on foot and by train before the train south: **Ghibli Museum (Mitaka)** in the morning, **teamLab Planets (Toyosu)** in the afternoon, and **Nintendo TOKYO (Shibuya)** as the early-evening cap. Book the Ghibli and teamLab timed tickets well ahead — both sell out.

**Schedule:** morning Ghibli Museum, Mitaka · lunch ~12:30 in Kichijōji (a short walk through Inokashira Park) · afternoon teamLab Planets, Toyosu · early-evening Nintendo TOKYO, Shibuya · dinner in Shibuya.

### Coffee — Kichijōji (near the Ghibli Museum walk)
- **LIGHT UP COFFEE** (4.4) — Kichijōji single-origin specialty roaster for a proper morning brew before or after Ghibli; cake and iced drinks for Aslan. https://maps.google.com/?cid=15630683996468648685

### Lunch — Kichijōji (walking distance from the Ghibli Museum)
- **Ramen Maji** (4.3) — popular local ramen bowl for Galiya, right by the museum walk. https://maps.google.com/?cid=4134068209230090282
- **Ibuki Udon Kichijoji** (4.2) — **[kid]** plain soft udon and rice sides Aslan will actually eat. https://maps.google.com/?cid=11889922840241589079
- **Uchoku** (4.4) — well-rated ramen alternative inside Coppice Kichijōji. https://maps.google.com/?cid=6124190306959042575

### Dinner — Shibuya (by Nintendo TOKYO)
- **ICHIRAN Shibuya** (4.4) — famous solo-booth Hakata tonkotsu ramen for Galiya. https://maps.google.com/?cid=1092125928485198446
- **Uobei Shibuya Dogenzaka** (4.3) — **[kid]** touch-screen conveyor sushi; order plain tamago, cucumber rolls, karaage and fries — fun and cheap for a 6-year-old. https://maps.google.com/?cid=5886746596086835007
- **Sushi to Rāmen Uogashiya Shibuya** (4.6) — sushi *and* ramen under one roof, serving the whole family. https://maps.google.com/?cid=15369335642700573936

### Things to do
- **~10:00 Ghibli Museum (Mitaka)** — Catbus, rooftop robot, members-only short film (advance Lawson tickets only).
- **~11:30 Inokashira Park** — a leafy walk and swan boats between the museum and Kichijōji lunch.
- **~14:00 teamLab Planets TOKYO (Toyosu)** — barefoot, wade-through immersive digital art.
- **~17:00 Nintendo TOKYO + Pokémon Center (Shibuya PARCO)** — Mario/Zelda/Splatoon flagship, near Shibuya Crossing, before dinner.
