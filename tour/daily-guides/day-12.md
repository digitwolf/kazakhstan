## Day 12 — Down to the Coast (Iya → Kōchi)

**Schedule:** depart Oboke/Iya ~09:00 · coffee at Otoyo michi-no-eki ~09:45 · Anpanman Museum (Kami) ~11:00 · lunch at Hirome Market ~13:00 · Kōchi Castle ~15:00 · Katsurahama ~16:30 · dinner in central Kōchi ~18:30.

The river valleys open out to the Pacific on a 75-mi / ~3 h riding day. Break the descent with coffee at Otoyo, swing east to the Anpanman Museum in Kami for Aslan, then roll into Kōchi for a late lunch at the famous Hirome Market — and spend the afternoon on the original castle keep and Katsurahama beach before a relaxed Tosa dinner.

### Lunch — Hirome Market, central Kōchi
- **Myojinmaru — Hirome Market Store** (4.2) — *katsuo no tataki* seared over flaming rice straw, Kōchi's signature dish eaten minutes after it's charred. Galiya & Ruslan. https://maps.google.com/?cid=685507622552095731
- **Hirome Market** (4.1, kid pick) — the raucous covered food hall itself: karaage, fries, plain rice and gyoza stalls mean Aslan finds something easy while the others graze. https://maps.google.com/?cid=11069022924402658269
- **Teuchi Udon Fujiya** (4.4, kid pick) — a quieter handmade-udon sit-down near the castle with plain udon for Aslan if the market is too much. https://maps.google.com/?cid=17212162267710166824

### Dinner — central Kōchi (Obiyamachi / Harimayachō)
- **Tosaryori Tsukasa** (4.1) — proper Tosa cuisine and *sawachi* sharing platters of sashimi, sushi and seared bonito. Galiya. https://maps.google.com/?cid=13999983515317910621
- **Ramen shop Kuraki** (4.3, kid pick) — Galiya's noodle fix with a plain bowl and gyoza that work for Aslan. https://maps.google.com/?cid=14938791122352366488
- **Genzo Obiyamachi** (4.1, kid pick) — central tonkatsu shop; crisp katsu and rice, a reliable easy meal for the picky eater. https://maps.google.com/?cid=8199549859830906235

### Things to do
- **Otoyo michi-no-eki** (~09:45) — first coffee/leg-stretch coming off the Iya mountains.
- **Anpanman Museum / Yanase Takashi Memorial Hall**, Kami (~11:00) — the home-town museum of Japan's beloved toddler superhero; a near-guaranteed delight for Aslan.
- **Kōchi Castle** (~15:00) — climb the original 1747 keep (one of only 12 surviving) and tour the lord's palace below.
- **Katsurahama** (~16:30) — crescent Pacific beach (look-don't-swim — strong currents), the Sakamoto Ryōma statue and a small aquarium.

*Moto-family option:* the **Shikoku Automobile Museum** in Kōnan (~30 bikes & ~30 cars, 1960s–80s) sits right by the Anpanman stop on the approach — but it's closed Mon **and** Tue, so check the day of the week before detouring.
