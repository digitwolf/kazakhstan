## Day 24 — Reposition to Tokyo

**Overnight:** Shinagawa / Haneda, Tokyo (last night before the homebound flight)

**Schedule:** A relaxed buffer day that also moves you back to Tokyo. Spend the morning at **Fushimi Inari** in Kyoto walking the thousand vermilion torii, then head to **Kyoto Station** for lunch and last souvenirs. With the travel cases already forwarded ahead by takkyubin, board an **afternoon Nozomi (~2h15m)** to Tokyo — grab right-side D/E seats for the **Mt Fuji** view near Shizuoka — and settle near **Shinagawa/Haneda** by about 17:00 for an easy last dinner and a repack.

### Coffee — Higashiyama, Kyoto (~early morning)
- **% ARABICA Kyoto Higashiyama** (4.4) — Kyoto's marquee third-wave specialty roaster, with picture-perfect lattes a few steps from the Yasaka Pagoda. A worthy last-morning coffee before Fushimi Inari for Galiya & Ruslan; a warm milk or juice keeps Aslan happy. https://maps.google.com/?cid=5927226485630185275

### Lunch — Fushimi Inari / Kyoto Station (~12:00)
- **Teuchi-udon Kendonya** (4.5) — hand-cut Kyoto udon & soba, a short walk from the Fushimi Inari gates. *Galiya.* https://maps.google.com/?cid=16984350875559884345
- **Inari Myoudai Dohachi** (4.7) — *kid pick:* kitsune/inari udon, the sweet fried-tofu noodle bowl that is the shrine's own dish — mild and easy for Aslan. https://maps.google.com/?cid=3500343508728031195
- **Katsukura - Kyoto Porta** (4.4) — *kid pick:* crisp tonkatsu sets with plain rice, right inside Kyoto Station so you can eat and board. https://maps.google.com/?cid=2658070174394966790

### Dinner — Shinagawa / Haneda (~18:30)
- **Ramen Waka Tokyo** (4.1) — a classic Tokyo shoyu ramen bowl steps from Shinagawa Station. *Galiya.* https://maps.google.com/?cid=13477905415039930785
- **Maruhachi** (4.4) — *kid pick:* crunchy katsu cutlet + plain rice, an easy safe meal for Aslan on the last night. https://maps.google.com/?cid=188921099918380869
- **Sushi Banya Kai Shinagawa** (4.2) — a celebratory last-night Tokyo sushi spot for Galiya & Ruslan. https://maps.google.com/?cid=14932386608607633121

*Tiny-eater backup:* **Kura Sushi Shinagawa Station** is right at the station — touch-screen conveyor-belt sushi with plenty of plain options and a fun kid factor if you'd rather end the trip with a relaxed grab-what-you-want dinner.

### Things to do
- **Fushimi Inari Taisha** (~09:00) — the iconic torii tunnels; go early to beat crowds, then ride straight to Kyoto Station from Inari.
- **Kyoto Station / Kyoto Porta** (~11:30) — underground food + shops for a last-minute snack and souvenirs before the train.
- **Nozomi Shinkansen to Tokyo** (~14:00) — book right-side seats for the Mt Fuji view; a fun finale leg for Aslan.
- **Repack near Shinagawa/Haneda** (~17:30) — cases already forwarded; just consolidate for the flight and have a calm last dinner.
