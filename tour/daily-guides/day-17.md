## Day 18 — Island Rest Day (overnight: Onomichi, Hiroshima)

A no-pressure rest day in the Setouchi: a gentle morning ride across to **Setoda on Ikuchijima** for the gilt-and-marble **Kōsanji** temple and the white-marble **Hill of Hope**, lemon gelato along the waterfront, then back to **Onomichi** for the day's anchor — a sit-down bowl of **Onomichi ramen** — and an easy afternoon on the **Senkō-ji ropeway**, temple lanes and cat alley, with harbour downtime at **Onomichi U2**.

**Schedule:** leisurely start ~09:30 · Setoda Kōsanji & Hill of Hope ~10:30 · lemon-gelato snack ~11:30 · Onomichi ramen ~13:00 · ropeway + temple walk ~15:00 · U2 / arcade ~16:30 · dinner ~18:30.

### Coffee — Onomichi (Tsuchidō arcade)
- **POUR specialty coffee** — third-wave specialty roaster (4.7, 590+ reviews) — *Galiya & Ruslan*: Onomichi's flagship specialty café in the covered arcade; great before the morning ride or as an afternoon U2/arcade stop, with juice and cake for Aslan. https://maps.google.com/?cid=7090208965271270685

### Snack — Setoda, Ikuchijima
- **Dolce Kosanjimae Store** — Setoda lemon gelato (4.1) — *Aslan*: bright lemon gelato right by Kōsanji. https://maps.google.com/?cid=9641240730542412151
- **尾道柑橘工房 SETODA** — citrus-workshop café (4.9) — *Galiya*: lemon cake, juice and a waterfront coffee. https://maps.google.com/?cid=13595938065999504966

### Lunch — Onomichi (the anchor meal)
- **Maruboshi** — Onomichi ramen (4.1, 2,300+ reviews) — *Galiya*: the iconic, always-queued bowl of the town's defining soy-niboshi ramen. https://maps.google.com/?cid=6892398988895765996
- **Shoya Hanten** — Onomichi ramen + gyoza & karaage (4.5) — *Aslan (kid)*: mild soy broth, plain flat noodles, gyoza and karaage. https://maps.google.com/?cid=14662320892713452271
- **Komedoko Shokudo** — teishoku / katsu sets (4.1) — *Aslan (kid)*: calm shokudo with katsu and plain rice as a no-queue backup. https://maps.google.com/?cid=17619095989743617765

### Dinner — Onomichi waterfront
- **Onomichi Yokocho** — Setouchi seafood food-alley (4.7) — *Galiya & kid*: fresh Inland-Sea seafood plus karaage/rice stalls Aslan can pick from. https://maps.google.com/?cid=4200612190604226638
- **Tamaganzo** — seafood izakaya (4.0) — *Galiya & Ruslan*: local catch and sake in a friendly harbour izakaya. https://maps.google.com/?cid=2391970382022306045
- **Sushi Kin** — sushi / conveyor-style (4.4) — *Aslan (kid)*: pick-your-own sushi, tamago and plain rolls. https://maps.google.com/?cid=10638192374241250106

### To do
- ~10:30 **Kōsanji & Hill of Hope (Setoda)** — ornate temple, cave walk-through and white-marble hilltop, a fun maze for a 6-year-old.
- ~11:30 **Setoda lemon stroll** — gelato and citrus stands on Japan's lemon island, flat and easy.
- ~15:00 **Senkōji Ropeway & Temple Walk** — cable-car up, classic bridge view, then wander down through halls, cafés and the cats.
- ~16:30 **Onomichi U2 / harbour arcade** — warehouse cycle-and-café complex and the covered shōtengai; also the wet-weather plan.
