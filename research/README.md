# PNW First-Tour — Research Library

A reference library for the **7-day Pacific Northwest family motorcycle "first tour"**
(Woodinville WA → Washington & Oregon coast → home over Mt. St. Helens & Mt. Rainier, **Jul 1–7
2026**). It is built around one fact: **Galiya just earned her endorsement and this is her first
multi-day tour as a solo rider**, on a small-tank **Kawasaki W230**, with **Ruslan carrying
6-year-old Aslan** on a **BMW R1300GS** — **own bikes, no rentals, no freeways, one ferry.**

These are background reference docs (not rendered on the site). Facts are cited inline; **verify
live before relying** (ferry fares, pass status, timed-entry rules and closures change).

## Headline takeaways for this trip
- **The new rider + the W230 govern everything.** Pace, day length and especially **fuel cadence** are set by Galiya and her ~3.4-gal tank — top off early/often (Hood Canal, the Coast Range, before Packwood & the Rainier finale). No freeways, no two long days back-to-back, no riding after dark.
- **The roads escalate nicely** from Easy (ferry, Hood Canal, flat coast) → Moderate (US-101 capes, SR-504 sweepers, Paradise) → the **Chinook Pass graduation ride** on Day 7. Nothing harder than "spirited at a calm pace."
- **Two seasonal/access watches:** **Chinook Pass (SR-410) is a seasonal pass** — normally open by early July but **confirm WSDOT before Day 7** (SR-7/Enumclaw is the fallback); and **Mount Rainier needs a summer timed-entry reservation** for Paradise — book it ahead. Also note **SR-504's upper road has been closed since a 2023 slide.**
- **Pack for three climates in one week:** cold/foggy coast → hot valley → cold alpine pass. Layers + rain shell for everyone, and a warm/waterproof layer for Aslan.
- **The own-bike, domestic loop simplifies the paperwork:** no passport, IDP or rental contracts — just endorsement, registration, insurance, the pre-trip service, and the child-pillion rig tested before Day 1.
- **Best on-route family wins:** Tillamook Creamery + Air Museum (Day 3, triple win), the Astoria Column & Maritime Museum (Day 2), Sea Lion Caves + Heceta Head on the rest day, and the Mount St. Helens story (Days 5–6).
