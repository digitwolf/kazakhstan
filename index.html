    Riding Safely Together

    Safety & Comfort First

    The whole plan is shaped around the newer rider's confidence and the child passenger's wellbeing — not mileage.

    🛣️ Newer Rider

    • Rides near the front so her pace controls the group
    • Paved rural, prefectural & coastal roads
    • Avoid dense cities, wet twisties & weekend rider roads
    • No back-to-back long transfer days
    • No narrow mountain roads after dark

    🧒 Child Passenger

    • Properly fitting helmet, jacket, gloves, pants & boots
    • Top case / back pad and intercom preferred
    • Must reach pegs and sit securely
    • Stop every 60–90 minutes
    • Snacks, rain layers & spare gloves carried by lead rider

    ⚠️ Before You Go

    • Valid licence + 1949 Geneva IDP for each rider
    • Insurance that covers motorcycle riding
    • Confirm passenger & child passenger allowed on rental
    • Confirm large-bike availability — Osaka loop (pick-up = drop-off)
    • Avoid rainy season, August heat & winter mountains
