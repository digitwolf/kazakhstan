# Hero image — ChatGPT prompt pack

Instructions for generating the homepage hero banner (`assets/hero.jpg`).

> Note: the site currently ships a verified Wikimedia Commons photo as the hero (see `docs/hero-image-credit.md` if present). Use this pack when you want a generated cinematic hero instead.

## How to use
1. In ChatGPT (GPT-4o / DALL·E 3 image generation), paste **one** prompt below.
2. Ask for a **landscape** image — the largest wide size, **1792 × 1024**. (The site shows it as a full-width ~16:9 banner.)
3. Generate a few variations, pick the best, then **download and save it as `assets/hero.jpg`** (replace the existing file). Re-export at ~1600 px wide, quality ~80, to keep it well under ~600 KB.
4. Text sits *below* the banner on the page, so the image needs **no text or empty space for text** — make it a full, rich scene.

## Recommended prompt — cinematic photo (use this one)

> A wide cinematic landscape photograph in warm golden-hour light: a small group of adventure motorcycles on Suzuki DR650-style dual-sport bikes riding away from the camera along a winding gravel track high in the Tian Shan mountains of Central Asia. The lead rider crests a rise trailing a soft plume of dust; behind them the track switchbacks down toward a vast turquoise alpine lake with a cluster of white nomad yurts and grazing horses on the green jailoo pasture at its shore. Towering snow-capped 4,000-metre peaks with hanging glaciers rise across the valley under a dramatic, gently clouded evening sky. All riders in proper adventure gear and full helmets, luggage-light bikes (a support truck far behind on the track). Mood: vast, remote, exhilarating — the Silk Road on two wheels. Rich natural color, subtle film warmth, sharp detailed foreground, atmospheric haze with depth. Photorealistic, high detail, no text, no logos, no watermarks, no license plates.

**Aspect:** landscape, 1792 × 1024.

## Alternate prompt — desert-to-glacier split scene

> A wide cinematic landscape photograph at golden hour: an adventure motorcycle on a dirt piste in the foreground crossing an ochre Central-Asian desert, a huge singing sand dune and striped white-and-red badlands behind it — while far in the distance the scene rises to snow-capped Tian Shan glaciers under a cool blue sky, the whole frame reading as one journey from desert heat up to alpine ice. A faint dust trail marks two more riders behind. Riders in full ADV gear and helmets. Photorealistic, rich warm-to-cool color gradient across the frame, high detail, no text, no logos, no watermarks.

## Alternate prompt — vintage travel poster (different vibe)

> A vintage-style travel poster, flat illustrated shapes with clean gradients, of a Central-Asia adventure motorcycle tour. A stylized dual-sport motorcycle with a dust plume riding a curving mountain track. Layered silhouette bands: an ochre singing dune and striped badlands low in the frame, a turquoise alpine lake with three white yurts and horses, and jagged glaciated Tian Shan peaks on the horizon under a warm sunset sky of amber, coral and teal. Minimalist, bold, adventurous, screen-print texture, limited harmonious palette. No text, no lettering, no logos, no watermarks.
