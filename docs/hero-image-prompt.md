# Hero image — ChatGPT prompt pack

Instructions for generating the homepage hero banner (`assets/hero.jpg`).

## How to use
1. In ChatGPT (GPT-4o / DALL·E 3 image generation), paste **one** prompt below.
2. Ask for a **landscape** image — the largest wide size, **1792 × 1024**. (The site shows it as a full-width ~16:9 banner; current file is 1672 × 941.)
3. Generate a few variations, pick the best, then **download and save it as `assets/hero.jpg`** (replace the existing file). Re-export at ~1600 px wide, quality ~80, to keep it well under ~600 KB.
4. Text sits *below* the banner on the page, so the image needs **no text or empty space for text** — make it a full, rich scene.

## Recommended prompt — cinematic photo (use this one)

> A wide cinematic landscape photograph in warm golden-hour light: two motorcycles riding away from the camera along a curving Pacific Northwest coastal highway. In front, a smaller classic retro-standard motorcycle ridden by a woman; just behind her, a large adventure-touring motorcycle ridden by a man with a small child riding pillion — all three in proper motorcycle jackets, gloves and full helmets. The two-lane road hugs a rugged Oregon coastline: a tall sea stack rising out of the glittering Pacific on the left, dark evergreen forest on the right. Far in the distance a snow-capped volcano (Mount Rainier) glows soft pink under a dramatic, gently clouded evening sky. Mood: hopeful, aspirational, the joyful start of a family road trip. Rich natural color, subtle film warmth, sharp detailed foreground, atmospheric haze with depth, shallow late-day shadows. Photorealistic, high detail, no text, no logos, no watermarks, no license plates.

**Aspect:** landscape, 1792 × 1024.

## Alternate prompt — vintage travel poster (different vibe)

> A vintage-style national-park travel poster, flat illustrated shapes with clean gradients, of a Pacific Northwest family motorcycle tour. Two stylized motorcycles — a small classic standard bike in front and a large adventure bike with a child passenger behind — riding a curving coastal highway. Layered silhouette bands: a tall sea stack and the glittering Pacific, dark evergreen ridges, and a snow-capped volcano on the horizon under a warm sunset sky of amber, coral and teal. Minimalist, bold, optimistic, screen-print texture, limited harmonious palette. No text, no lettering, no logos, no watermarks.

**Aspect:** landscape, 1792 × 1024.

## Getting the bikes / details right (tips for iterating)
- Image models won't render exact brands — don't name Kawasaki/BMW. Describe them by character: a **small retro/classic standard** (Galiya's W230) in front; a **tall adventure-touring bike with luggage** (Ruslan's R1300GS) carrying a **small child pillion** behind. This keeps the family story without brand artifacts.
- If the child looks off, ask for "a small child passenger sitting securely behind the adult, both in full helmets."
- If it adds text or signs, regenerate with "absolutely no text, signage, or letters anywhere."
- Want it to lean coast vs. mountains? Swap emphasis: "ocean and sea stack dominant" or "Mount Rainier large on the horizon."
- Optional extra scenery to weave in (pick one, don't crowd): a coastal lighthouse, a state-ferry on the water (Day 1), or wildflower meadows below the peak (Day 7).

## Avoid (negative cues)
text · letters · captions · watermarks · logos · brand badges · license plates · distorted/extra limbs · more than two motorcycles · cluttered composition · oversaturated/HDR look.
