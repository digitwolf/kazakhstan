## Osaka bookend — the toy & figure mecca (the richest haul for Aslan)

### Den Den Town / Nipponbashi (でんでんタウン) **[OSAKA BOOKEND: Days 1, 21]**
- **What & why it fits:** Osaka's otaku/electronics district — the **single best toy & figure
  shopping on the trip**. For this family:
  - **Figures & collectibles:** **Mandarake**, **Hobby Station**, Volks, Jungle and many figure/
    model shops — anime figures, Gunpla/model kits, trading cards, vintage toys.
  - **Transformers / robots:** Japanese **Takara Tomy Transformers** (and Diaclone/robot mecha) turn
    up in the figure shops and second-hand stores here — Den Den Town is the most likely place on the
    whole loop to find Transformers and transforming-robot toys. *No single guaranteed
    Transformers-only store; browse Mandarake/Hobby shops. Verify current stock — retail churns.*
  - **Gachapon (capsule toys):** floors of capsule machines — cheap, repeatable souvenir fun.
  - **Super Potato** retro-game shop (also in [nintendo-mario.md](nintendo-mario.md)).
- **Fit:** an Osaka bookend afternoon; sits near **Kuromon Ichiba** food market and within Osaka's
  Namba/Nipponbashi core — easy to combine food + toys + Nintendo in one outing.
- Source: https://www.kansaibeyond.com/ (Den Den Town guides)

### Daimaru Umeda 13F — Nintendo Osaka + Pokémon Center **[OSAKA BOOKEND]**
- Character-goods one-stop (see [nintendo-mario.md](nintendo-mario.md)) — plush and figures for

### Other Osaka character/toy stops **[OSAKA BOOKEND]**

## Transformers — what actually exists (verify before relying)
- There is **no dedicated Transformers museum/park** in Japan that fits this loop. Transformers in
  Japan is **Takara Tomy** (the original co-creator) — the best ways to engage it on this trip:
  1. **Den Den Town figure/second-hand shops** (Osaka, above) — the most reliable place to *buy*
     Transformers/robot toys on-route.
  2. **Tokyo bookend:** larger Transformers/Takara Tomy retail and the occasional anime-store
     Transformers section concentrate in **Akihabara / Nakano Broadway** — a Day-0/Day-22 option.
  3. **Pop-ups & events** (Tokyo Toy Show, anniversary pop-ups) are date-dependent — **search for
     current Transformers pop-ups near the travel dates closer to departure**; nothing permanent to
     route around.
- *Treat any specific Transformers retail as "reconfirm before relying" — toy retail changes fast.*

## KPop Demon Hunters / K-pop tie-ins (Aslan) — honest status
- **KPop Demon Hunters** is a film property, not a Japan-based attraction — **no permanent Japanese
  venue or museum exists.** Any tie-in would be a **temporary pop-up, café collaboration or
  merchandise drop**, which are date- and city-specific (most likely Tokyo or Osaka).
- **Action:** **search for "KPop Demon Hunters pop-up / café Japan" closer to the travel dates** —
  if one is running in Osaka or Tokyo during the trip, it becomes a bookend option. K-pop merch
  generally is easy to find in Osaka/Tokyo (K-pop goods shops, Shin-Ōkubo in Tokyo is the K-pop
  district). **Nothing to route around now; flag as a near-departure check.**
- *No reliable permanent source to cite — this is a watch-item, reconfirm near departure.*
